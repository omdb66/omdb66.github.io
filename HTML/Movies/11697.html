Denis does not have a job nor any sense for responsibility. Charlotte is on her way to be a police woman and is indeed aware of her obligations and duties. Two young adults out of two completely different worlds get to clash in old Raimund Groenert's house, both having their own intentions in the back of their minds. Charlotte takes care of her grandfather Raimund, who needs a wheelchair due to a recent accident. Denis on the other side breaks into the property with an eye on valuable possessions, impersonating a nurse. None of them wants to spend a lot of time in there until a giant snow storm prevents them from leaving.
