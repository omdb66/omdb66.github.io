Here is the BluRay-based release of the 25 Year Concert in Budokan, dedicated to collaborative work of Joe Hisaishi and Studio Ghibli, conducted by the maestro himself against the backdrop of the films. With New Japan Philharmonic World Dream Orchestra, Ritsuyukai Choir, The Little Singers of TOKYO and many more. On August 4th and 5th, the event what was originally planned to be two performances turned into three to meet the raving demands of those couldn’t get get tickets in time. The event featured performances by Hisaishi, a 200 piece orchestra, and a 400 piece choir.
