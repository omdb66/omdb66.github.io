#9572 Lego Bionicle: The Journey to One (Serien-Übersicht)

Produziert: 2016


Sechs legendäre Helden begeben sich auf eine epische Mission, bei der sie die Kraft der Naturelemente nutzen, um die Kräfte des Bösen aufzuhalten.

Season01

Episode01: Prolog: Die Legende beginnt

Meet the warriors who wield the power of the elements to protect Okoto Island, as they prepare for a bold new quest in search of an ancient mask.

Episode02: Pflicht und Einheit

To stop Makuta, the Toa must first split up to find the six Golden Masks of Unity and forge a bond with the island's ancient elemental creatures.

Episode03: Der Weg zur Maske von Makuta

As the Toa set out to find the labyrinth holding Makuta's mask, Umarak the hunter follows close behind and discovers a key weakness in the group.

Season02

Episode01: Zerstörerisches Spiel

Drawing new power from the Mask of Control, Umarak raises an army of beasts to keep the Toa busy while he tries to open a portal to the shadow realm.

Episode02: Das dunkle Portal

At the Black Crater, the Toa discover their true destiny as they take on the Destroyer and Makuta in a final showdown.
