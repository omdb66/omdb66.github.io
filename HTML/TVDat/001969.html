#1413 Appleseed XIII (Serien-Übersicht)

Produziert: 1988-2014


Der 5. Weltkrieg war ein globaler Konflikt, in dem diverse Nationen erbittert gegeneinander kämpften und so die Weltbevölkerung halbierten - Was blieb, ist ein vollkommen zerstörter Planet und ein Stadt-Staat namens "Olympus", dessen Frieden es um jeden Preis zu verteidigen gilt! Olympus ist eine futuristische Metropole, die von der mächtigen künstlichen Intelligenz "Gaia" regiert und von gentechnisch veränderten, menschenähnlichen "Bioroids" verwaltet wird. Auf den ersten Blick wirkt alles friedlich, doch der Schein trügt und jede Menge rassistische, religiöse und politische Konflikte bedrohen den Frieden der Stadt.

Season01

Episode01: Der nemëische Löwe

Deunan, Briareos, and the rest of the Olympus's ES.W.A.T force fend off an Argonaut attack.

Episode02: Die Ställe des Augias

Deunan and Briareos are sent to end a hostage situation on a plane with 3000 passengers. But when they arrive the entire plane is eerily empty.

Episode03: Die kerynitische Hirschkuh

A surge in bioroid suicides has caused a stir throughout Olympus. Deunan steps in to investigate this mysterious situation.

Episode04: Die Rinder des Geryon

A Kottos robot on the police force malfunctions in an unexpected way after meeting a little girl.

Episode05: Der kretische Stier

While on a security assignment, members of ES.W.A.T. are infected by something that cause madness and tremendous bloodthirst. Not even Deunan is immune to its effects.

Episode06: Der erymanthische Eber

Deunan embarks on a solo mission to infiltrate the Argonauts and learn more about an enormous and extremely high-tech Landmate.

Episode07: Die stymphalischen Vögel

As the Stymphalean Landmate approaches Olympus with a nuclear warhead, Briareos and ES.W.A.T. rush to diffuse the situation.

Episode08: Die Pferde des Diomedes

Deunan is assigned to provide security to a Poseidon ambassador of goodwill aboard a submarine. But things go awry when the submarine malfunctions and other passengers begin to die mysteriously.

Episode09: Der Gürtel der Hippolyte

A goodwill ambassador arrives from Poseidon... except she looks and sounds exactly like Deia, who should be dead.

Episode10: Die Lernäische Hydra

Deunan and Briareos are assigned to protect a bioroid sculptor from Deia, who is bent on killing a specific group of them.

Episode11: Die Äpfel der Hesperiden

ES.W.A.T. is dispatched to the Argonaut to provide security for the final bioroid on Deia's hit list. Little does anyone know Deia has already made the first move.

Episode12: Der Höllenhund Kerberos

Deunan and Briareos face off with Deia in a final battle.

Episode13: Paradeisos

Some time after the Deia incident, ES.W.A.T. is called in to supervise a diagnostic check of the Gaia Computer's systems. Soon after, unmanned Landmates and Auto Insectors start to malfunction.

Specials

Episode01: Appleseed (OVA)

In the futuristic metropolis of Olympus a terrorist attack takes place. A special ESWAT team, including Deunan Knute and Briareos Hecatonchires, is sent in to deal with the threat. They succeed in averting the immediate danger, but one of the terrorists manages to escape.The OVA tells the story of a terrorist plot to take over Gaia — the central AI running Olympus. This involves a disillusioned ESWAT officer and the terrorist who got away, and Hitomi, Deunan's bioroid friend who is used as a pawn to gain access to Gaia.

Episode02: Appleseed

In 2131 AD Deunan Knute has been a soldier on countless battlefields for most of her life. When the World War ends, she is transported to the city of Olympus to live peacefully and finds out that, in an effort to protect the human race from future wars, humans have created the Bioroids, a genetically enhanced race of people that have restrained feelings and therefore cannot hurt each other.She discovers, however, that not everyone is happy with this Human-Bioroid symbiosis: Many humans treat Bioroids as a lower species and as a threat to their future. The human-controlled military is seeking to annihilate all Bioroids, and the Bioroid-controlled government is afraid of a coup d'état.The task falls on Deunan to control the situation, and to avert the inter-species war. In this she is aided by her former lover, Briareos, who has lost his body in war, to become a cyborg — a brain in an artificial body.
