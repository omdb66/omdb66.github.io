#3930 Night Manager, The (Serien-Übersicht)

Produziert: 2016


Der ehemalige britischen Soldate Jonathan Pine arbeitet als Nachtmanager in einem Luxushotel. Als ihn der britische Geheimdienst rekrutiert, um dem Waffenhändler Richard Onslow Roper das Handwerk zu legen, ist Pine mehr als gewillt, wieder in den Dienst zurückzukehren. Schließlich ist Roper verantwortlich für den grausamen Mord an Pines Freundin Sophie. Um sie zu rächen und einen der gefährlichsten Männer unserer Zeit zu Fall zu bringen, muss er sein Leben riskieren und den Kreis von Ropers Vertrauten infiltrieren, darunter auch Ropers Geliebte Jed. Doch schon bald zeigt sich, dass Rache nicht das einzige ist, was Pine antreibt ...

Season01

Episode01: Episode 1

Hotel night manager Jonathan Pine receives a plea for help from a well-connected guest. His actions draw him into the world of Richard Roper, a businessman and arms dealer.

Episode02: Episode 2

On the Mediterranean island of Mallorca, Roper’s life of luxury and calm is shattered. Six months earlier, Burr continues her recruitment of Pine, sending him to Devon to build his cover story.

Episode03: Episode 3

While he continues to recuperate in Roper’s villa, Pine starts to dig up secrets about the other members of the household. Meanwhile, Burr and Steadman seize on an opportunity to recruit a new asset.

Episode04: Episode 4

Roper welcomes Pine into his inner circle, leaving Corky out in the cold.Meanwhile, Burr has concerns for the safety of her source, when she suspects key information has been leaked to the River House.

Episode05: Episode 5

A suspicious Roper gathers his entourage around him in an attempt to root out the traitor, forcing Pine to play a dangerous game. In London, Burr and Steadman face mounting opposition from Whitehall.

Episode06: Episode 6

Roper and his team return to Cairo for the deal, reuniting Pine with an old enemy. Pine risks it all to put his plan in motion. A discredited Burr makes one last stand.
