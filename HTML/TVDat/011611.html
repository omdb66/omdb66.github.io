#9571 Lego Friends: The Power of Friendship (Serien-Übersicht)

Produziert: 2016

Five best friends face adventures side by side in their hometown. Zany antics, love and missteps are better with friends!

Season01

Episode01: Camp Wild Hearts

The Friends' fun summer camp vacation is threatened by the Mayor's plan to tear down the camp. Meanwhile, Mia wonders if being a winner at sports will make her a loser at romance.

Episode02: Slam Dunk

The Friends' basketball team, the Streaks, is in danger of getting cut if they can't find a coach and a replacement for an injured teammate. The answer comes from a surprising source, and leads to a battle for the championship against Tanya's team.

Episode03: Keepin' It Real

The Friends hit on an awesome idea to save their webcast: make a rock documentary about Heartlake City's own mega-pop star, Livi. But their zany antics nearly bring about the end of Livi's career -- and threaten to split up the Friends forever.

Episode04: Roomies

Stephanie must share her room for a whole week with her arch-rival Tanya. Worse, when Tanya is injured, Stephanie is forced to play nursemaid to the demanding diva. Or could Tanya be faking the whole thing?

Season02

Episode01: Keepin' It Real

To save their webcast, the friends shoot a documentary about pop star Livi. But they have trouble sticking to their pledge to keep things honest.

Episode02: Roomies

Stephanie's week goes from bad to worse when she's forced to share a room with her biggest rival: the spoiled and snooty Tanya.
